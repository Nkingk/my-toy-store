Our Heritage

Founded in a small Copenhagen workshop in 1982 by master carpenter Lars Jensen, Teddy & Co. was born out of a simple frustration: the rise of brittle, mass-produced plastic toys that lacked soul and durability.

Lars believed that toys should be tactile, safe, and beautiful enough to pass down to grandchildren. Today, we maintain his rigorous standards, employing traditional joinery techniques and hand-finishing every piece.

Uncompromising Materials

We source 100% of our timber from FSC-certified sustainable European forests. Our plush companions are stitched from Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) certified cotton, ensuring zero exposure to harmful pesticides.

Instead of toxic varnishes, we use natural beeswax and food-grade linseed oils. We don't just build toys; we build safe, tactile environments for cognitive development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we provide fully tracked, climate-neutral shipping to over 40 countries worldwide. European deliveries take 2-4 days, while international freight takes 5-10 business days.
Are the dyes completely safe if chewed? +
Absolutely. We strictly use plant-based, water-soluble pigments and seal our wooden toys with organic beeswax. They meet and exceed all European (EN71) and US (ASTM F963) safety standards.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day "no questions asked" return window for any unused items in their original packaging. We also offer a lifetime warranty against structural manufacturing defects.
